basic

在字符串中引用符号，需要加\

特殊字符：

  • \n newline
  • \t tab

String length

str_length(c("a", "R for data science", NA))
## [1]  1 18 NA

Combining strings

str_c("x", "y")
## [1] "xy"
str_c("x", "y", "z")
## [1] "xyz"
str_c("x", "y", sep = ", ")
## [1] "x, y"

将所要合并的向量作为第一个参数传入，需要用到collapse参数

str_c(1:10,collapse = "")
## [1] "12345678910"

str_c是向量化的，对于短向量会自动循环补齐

str_c("prefix-", c("a", "b", "c"), "-suffix")
## [1] "prefix-a-suffix" "prefix-b-suffix" "prefix-c-suffix"

Subsetting strings

str_sub取字符串的闭区间（即包括开始和结束）

x <- c("Apple", "Banana", "Pear")
str_sub(x, 1, 3)
## [1] "App" "Ban" "Pea"

负数，则从后往前数，但仍是左在前，右在后

str_sub(x,-3,-1)
## [1] "ple" "ana" "ear"

字符串过短时，str_sub也不会崩溃

str_sub("a", 1, 5)
## [1] "a"

str_sub可以原地修改

str_sub(x, 1, 1) <- str_to_lower(str_sub(x, 1, 1))
x
## [1] "apple"  "banana" "pear"

Locales

  • str_to_lower()
  • str_to_upper()
  • str_to_title()

Matching patterns with regular expressions

为了学习正则表达式，使用str_view和str_view_all

x <- c("apple", "banana", "pear")
str_view(x, "an")

.可以匹配任意字符（除了空行）

str_view(x, ".a.")

如果要匹配. 前面还需再加个\.，但为了正则表达式，还需要再加个\.

str_view(c("abc", "a.c", "bef"), "a\\.c")

如果要匹配\，需要四个斜杠

x <- "a\\b"
str_view(x, "\\\\")

Anchors

从开头或结尾开始匹配

  • \^ 从开头开始匹配
  • \$ 从结尾开始匹配
x <- c("apple", "banana", "pear")
str_view(x, "^a")
str_view(x, "a$")
